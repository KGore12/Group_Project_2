The Research

The data we are using comes from Kaggle. The dataset contains .csv files statistics for the 2016 and 2020 presidential election, COVID-19 cases and deaths, demographic numbers, and economic numbers. We narrowed our focus to the COVID-19 statistics and 2020 presidential election poll data. Is there a pattern showing a relationship to the number COVID-19 cases and deaths with each state's election poll data by partisanship?


Data source provided to the respective page with link for you to further explore yourself. Due to changes in data collection and reporting for both the 2020 presidential election and COVID-19, this is only a "snapshot" of the data sources up to November 1st, 2020.


Notes About the Data: The COVID variables are from November 1st, 2020, which was a couple days before the election. The most recent economic data obtained is from 2017.
